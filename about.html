Artificial Intelligence

AI (Artificial Intelligence) is a technology that mimics human intelligence through computer systems that simulate human cognitive abilities, learning abilities, and the ability to perform tasks. AI technologies are designed to enable computer systems to perceive environments, learn knowledge, make decisions, and perform tasks so as to achieve functions similar to those of human intelligence.

AI has a wide range of applications in various fields, including healthcare, finance, transportation, education, etc., and has had a profound impact on social and economic development.

AI4SG projects have grown in scope and popularity

In recent years, a large number of Artificial Intelligence (AI) projects have been deployed. In these, the aim is to utilize cutting-edge technologies to alleviate critical issues such as poverty, hunger, crime, and climate change under the broader ‘AI for Social Good’ (AI4SG) umbrella.

A quantitative analysis of approximately 900 studies on the topic of AI4SG conducted by an academic over the past decade showed that healthcare was the top research application area.

Mission

The mission and goals of Artificial Intelligence for Social Good (AI for Social Good) are usually to utilize artificial intelligence technologies to solve social problems such as poverty, healthcare, education, environmental protection, etc., to reduce social inequality, to promote social progress and to improve people's lives.
